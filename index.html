About Us

STEAMHers is an organization dedicated to empowering women in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM). Founded by Shreya Baronia and Maider Escobar, STEAMHers aims to create a supportive community for women to thrive in traditionally male-dominated fields.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide educational resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities to women and girls of all ages. We believe that by fostering a collaborative environment, we can inspire the next generation of female leaders in STEAM.

We aim to break down barriers and create a more inclusive STEAM community. Our initiatives are designed to empower women and girls with the skills and confidence they need to succeed in science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics.

Through our partnerships with schools, universities, and industry leaders, we strive to provide real-world experiences and connect our members with role models and mentors who can guide them on their journey.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world." - Nelson Mandela

Why We Started

The founders of STEAMHers, Shreya Baronia and Maider Escobar, both experienced challenges and discrimination in STEAM fields due to their gender.

Shreya often felt alienated in male-dominated environments, while Maider recognized the necessity of creating opportunities for women in areas where there were few opportunities for them to drive meaningful change.

Together, they launched STEAMHers to combat these issues and to create a platform where women can thrive and support each other.

Our Vision

We envision a world where women are equally represented in all areas of STEAM.

Through our advocacy and initiatives, we aim to break down barriers that hinder women's progress in STEAM, whether they be cultural, systemic, or institutional. We envision a future where young girls grow up knowing that they have a place in STEAM careers and where established professionals find support and opportunities to advance their careers and leadership roles.

Ultimately, our vision extends beyond equality to equity, where resources, opportunities, and recognition are distributed fairly among all genders, ensuring that no woman is left behind in the march towards scientific and technological advancement.

Our Programs

We offer a variety of programs including online courses, workshops, competitions, and summer programs. These initiatives are designed to build confidence, enhance skills, and promote gender equality in STEAM fields.

Our vision is a world where women are equally represented in all areas of STEAM, contributing their unique perspectives and talents to drive innovation and progress.

Over the Year Program

Our Over The Year Program offers a diverse range of courses and engaging competitions throughout the year, providing a continuous opportunity for girls to explore and deepen their knowledge in STEAM subjects.

Join us and take advantage of this year-round program to learn, explore, and create in our comprehensive STEAM classes. Discover new interests and develop skills that will pave the way for your future success!

Program Details:

  1. Duration: Courses are held throughout the year, typically in 1-2 hour sessions, allowing for thorough exploration of various STEAM topics.

  2. Frequency: Classes are scheduled at different times to accommodate busy schedules and ensure ongoing learning.

Please note that while we strive to offer a wide range of courses throughout the year, some classes may be subject to availability based on teacher schedules.

We are committed to ensuring a rich and fulfilling educational experience for all participants!

Advanced problem-solving techniques and competition preparation.

  • Focus on algebra, geometry, and number theory.

Geometry/Algebra 2/Trigonometry ▼

In-depth exploration of mathematical concepts and applications.

  • Geometry: Study of shapes, sizes, and properties of figures.
    • Euclidean and non-Euclidean geometry.
    • Properties of shapes, theorems, and proofs.
    • Coordinate geometry and transformations.
    • Geometric constructions and loci.
  • Algebra 2: Advanced algebraic concepts and techniques.
    • Polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions.
    • Complex numbers and their properties.
    • Systems of equations and inequalities.
    • Matrices and determinants.
  • Trigonometry:
    • Trigonometric functions and their applications.
    • Graphs of sine, cosine, and tangent functions.
    • Trigonometric identities and equations.
    • Law of Sines and Law of Cosines.
    • Applications in real-world contexts, such as physics and engineering.

STEAM Contests

We organize STEAM virtual competitions for girls and women, offering exciting prizes to the winners. These competitions are designed to inspire creativity, innovation, and a passion for STEAM subjects. Remember, "The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams." Let's make those dreams a reality together!

STEAMHers International Summer 2024 Computer Science Competition (Grades 6-8) ▼

  • 1. Contest Format: The contest contains 30 multiple-choice questions covering fundamental computer science knowledge typically taught at the secondary level. Participants will solve theoretical questions, with timed sessions to test quick thinking and problem-solving abilities.

  • 2. Topics Covered:

    • Binary system and data encoding.
    • Electronic components and their functions.
    • Nibbles, bytes, and basic data structures.
    • Introductory programming concepts in Python or Scratch.

  • 3. Skills Developed:

    • Algorithmic thinking and coding efficiency.
    • Understanding of fundamental computer science concepts.
    • Debugging and error correction.
    • Teamwork in pair programming exercises.

  • 4. Preparatory Resources:

    • Access to coding platforms and sample problems.

  • 5. Support and Prizes:

    • Prizes for top coders.
    • Opportunities to participate in advanced coding competitions.

STEAMHers International Summer 2024 Math Competition (Grades 6-8) ▼

A challenging math contest designed for middle schoolers.

  • 1. Contest Format: The contest consists of multiple-choice and open-ended questions. To enhance quick thinking, problem-solving sessions are timed.
